What are sports and why should we play them?

Sports are activitys that involve physical interaction and lots of skill where you competewith other people
to win. In most sports theres some sort of running where you must do something to score. For example in soccer
you run around and kick a ball to try and make it in a goal. One goal results in you scoring and gaining one point.
But, there are many other sports that require less physical ability if you want something less demanding too.
