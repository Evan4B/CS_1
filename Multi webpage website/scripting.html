Chocolate Chip Cookies

Chocolate chip cookies are probably the most iconic desert in the world. This is due to how they have turned into just a staple for a quick easy dessert. But they are much different than most people believe. They are actuallt extreamly difficult to make right and perfect. So how do you make the chocolate chip cookie perfect.

Source: Sheila Brown, Chocolate Chip Cookie And Milk, Publicdomainpictures.net, (https://publicdomainpictures.net/en/view-image.php?image=371723&picture=chocolate-chip-cookie-and-milk)

Ingredients

    1 cup butter, softened
    1 cup white sugar
    1 cup packed brown sugar
    2 eggs
    2 teaspoons vanilla extract
    1 teaspoon baking soda
    2 teaspoons hot water
    Â½ teaspoon salt
    3 cups all-purpose flour
    2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
    1 cup chopped walnuts

Directions

    1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
    2. Beat butter, white sugar, and brown sugar with an electric mixer in a large bowl until smooth. Beat in eggs, one at a time, then stir in vanilla. Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to batter along with salt. Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and walnuts.
    3. Drop spoonfuls of dough 2 inches apart onto ungreased baking sheets.
    4. Bake in the preheated oven until edges are nicely browned, about 10 minutes. Cool on the baking sheets briefly before removing to a wire rack to cool completely.
